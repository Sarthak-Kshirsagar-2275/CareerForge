About CareerPilot

CareerPilot is an AI-powered career guidance platform designed to help students explore career options, understand skill requirements, and create personalized learning roadmaps.

Our Mission

We aim to make career planning easier by providing clear, personalized guidance based on a student's interests, skills, education level, and goals.

What CareerPilot Offers

• Personalized career recommendations
• Step-by-step learning roadmaps
• Skill development suggestions
• Curated learning resources
• AI-powered career insights

Why We Built It

Many students struggle to decide what career path to pursue. CareerPilot was created to simplify that process and provide practical guidance that helps students make informed decisions.
